Got questions?
DB Access aur Managed Recruitment mein kya fark hai? +
DB Access mein aap khud candidates browse karke directly contact karte ho — ₹2999/month. Managed Recruitment mein hum sab kuch karte hain, aap sirf interview lete ho — ₹999 token + 7% CTC on hire.
7% CTC kab pay karna hota hai? +
Sirf tab jab candidate successfully hire ho jaaye aur joining confirm ho jaaye. ₹999 token amount final fee mein adjust ho jaata hai. Agar hire nahi hua, token wapas milta hai.
3-month replacement guarantee exactly kaise kaam karta hai? +
Agar hired candidate 3 mahine ke andar resign karta hai ya performance issues aate hain, hum notice period ke andar hi ek replacement candidate provide karenge — bilkul free, koi extra charge nahi.
High-Skill Verified Candidates add-on kya hota hai? +
Yeh Managed Recruitment ka premium add-on hai. Isme hum sirf un candidates ko consider karte hain jinke skill test scores top 20% mein hain, jinki background verification ho chuki hai, aur jo technically verified hain. Quality hiring ke liye best option.
Kya job posting plans aur DB Access saath use kar sakte hain? +
Bilkul! Dono independent hain. Aap Growth plan se 5 jobs post karo, aur DB Access se khud bhi candidates browse karo. Dono saath chalte hain.
